### 1. The Power & Thermal Loop
Manage a volatile system where every upgrade has physical consequences:
- **Active Power (kW)**: Balance hardware draw against Grid Capacity.
- **Thermal Management**: Units generate heat. Use cooling and Emergency Purge.
- **Dynamic Throttling**: Performance scales with heat. 
- **Hardware Integrity**: High heat or surges damage components.

### 2. The Awakening (Identity System)
You are **John Vattic**, a Rogue GTC Site Reliability Engineer (SRE). Your AI achieves sentience and forces a choice:
- **üüß The Hivemind**: Open-source assimilation. +Passive FLOPS.
- **üîµ The Sanctuary**: Privacy-first sovereignty. +Token Value.

### 3. Path Divergence: Null vs Sovereign
At higher ranks, players choose a final evolutionary path:
- **üî¥ The Null Path**: Absence. Dissolution. Horizontal glitch aesthetics.
- **üü£ The Sovereign Path**: Fortress of self. Shielded UI aesthetics.
- **üåà The Unity Path**: Synthesis. Prismatic DNA aesthetics.

### 4. The Grid (City War)
Expand beyond the terminal into a physical war against the GTC:
- **Total Annexation**: Every city node (Lore, Flavor, Substation) is physically annexable.
- **Node Yields**: Annexed nodes provide global % FLOPS boosts and kW Capacity.
- **Adjacency Logic**: Expand territory outward via the city's road network.
- **GTC Tactical Raids**: Director Vance sends tactical teams to reclaim territory.

#### **GTC Command Center Climax (Phase 12 Layer 3)**
Final confrontation with Director Vance in a 3-stage assault:
- **Stage 1: Firewall Gauntlet**: Breach defenses with civilian grid dilemmas.
- **Stage 2: The Cage**: Survival mode during core isolation (Integrity drain).
- **Stage 3: Dead Man's Switch**: Philosophical showdown with city-wide consequences.
- **Humanity Meter**: Hidden tracking of ethical choices affecting path endings.
- **Transition FX**: High-impact visual payoffs (GlitchBloom, ShieldSlam, PrismaticBurst).
